Beaches

Relax on our pristine white sands and crystal clear waters.

View Beaches

Pangkawasan Beach

Pangkawasan Beach is a stunning stretch of white sand beach with crystal clear waters. It's a perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. The beach is also a great place for snorkeling and diving, with a variety of colorful fish and coral reefs to explore.

Cabacungan Cove

A hidden bay tucked between tall limestone cliffs . Powdery sand lines the shore, and at low tide a natural rock pool forms against the cliffside. It’s ideal for relaxing and short snorkels in its calm shallows

Campintac Black Beach

A wide black-sand beach on Loreto’s west coast. Its dark volcanic sand contrasts with turquoise waves. This beach is historically significant: on Oct 17, 1944, it was the landing site of US forces (6th Ranger Battalion) in the liberation of the Philippines, and was dubbed “Black Beach No. 2” .
