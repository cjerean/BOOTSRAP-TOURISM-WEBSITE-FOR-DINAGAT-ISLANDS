Pangabangan Island “Blue Lagoon”

A small heart-shaped lagoon sheltered by limestone; famed for clear turquoise water and snorkeling (turtles, reef sharks) . It’s reached by boat from Loreto or via island-hopping tours.

Lake Bababu

A rare freshwater lake connected via an underground tunnel to the sea (650 m long) . It sits behind dense forest and is reached by a 30-minute trek . The lake’s top layer is pure rainwater over salt water, and a hidden cove lies at its edge . Birdwatchers may spot kingfishers or herons here.

Bitaog Beach

Fine white sand and calm blue sea make this a popular swimming spot. There is little shade, so sunbathing is common. The beach is lightly developed (no big resorts), so expect basic or no facilities .
